Bajrangdas Lohiya Central Library

The Bajrangdas Lohiya Central Library of Vishwakarma Institute of Technology was established in 1984. The main object of Bajrangdas Lohiya Central library is to support the educational & research programmes of the Institute by providing physical and intellectual access to information, consistent with the present and the anticipated educational and research functions of the institute. The Bajrangdas Lohiya Central library consists of Reference Section, Journal Section, Reading Hall and Stack-Room. The library has unique collection of Encyclopedia, Handbooks, Textbooks, Journals, Video Cassettes, CD'S, B.I.Standards, Video courses etc. The Bajrangdas Lohiya Central library uses SLIM 21 software. This software consists of modules of Acquisitions, Cataloguing Circulation, Serial Control, Article Indexing and WEB OPAC. Circulation system of books is based on Bar-Code.
